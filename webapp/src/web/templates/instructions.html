Instructions

  • Look at your view on the right.
  • You and your partner will take turns, and try to agree on one dot that you both have in common.
  • We will give you an additional $0.25 bonus if you and your partner successfully find one object in common.
  • You will be playing with a human or robot.
  • The robot may be a bit ambiguous. Say yes if their question applies to any dots!
    • In a turn, you can type a message in the chat box below and press Enter to send it, or click a dot to select it.
    • Your partner will not see which dot you select, and you won't see their selection, so try to communicate well in the messages!
    • You can select anytime after 1 minute, but make very sure that you found one object in common!
    • Please use natural sentences as much as possible, and do not talk about things that are irrelevant to the task.
  • Please be aware that we will not accept chats which (i) did not follow instructions or (ii) did not make a good attempt to succeed. If you find problems with the task, please return and do not submit that HIT.
